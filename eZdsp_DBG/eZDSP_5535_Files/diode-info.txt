eZDSP LED Map:

DS2:  XF (xtra I/O) - Bit 13 of ST1_55 Register.  Diode DS2.  (Small Green LED) - active high

DS6:  GPIO 14                                                 (BLUE) - active low
DS5:  GPIO 15                                                 (Yellow) - active low
DS4:  GPIO 16                                                 (RED) - active low
DS3:  GPIO 17                                                 (Green) - active low

DS1:  PWREN# of FT2232HL - Green - should be on if USB power is on.
note DS2 is adjacent and inboard of DS1.

SW1, SW2 => GPAIN1
